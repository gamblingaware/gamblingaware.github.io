    Gambling Starts Young

    Our survey has indicated the proportionate amount of students and younger people that gamble within Australia.

    ● Mostly through casual gambling with friends and/or family.
    ● Consider the effect causal gambling with friends or family has on younger people.
    ● Younger people should be aware of how this type of gambling can trigger future issues.
    ● Parents should be aware of their children’s participation in this type of gambling.

    ● Gambling advertisements are found everywhere, but, particularly in sports broadcasts
    ● Parents should be aware of what their children are getting exposed to during these broadcasts
    ● Gambling advertisements could influence children to gamble, particularly if its on their favourite sport

    Gambling Advertisements are Everywhere

    Our survey found that 59% of people had regularly seen gambling advertisements
    A survey of 205 people was conducted to discover some of the key issues surrounding youth gambling, the team was particularly interested in finding out what the causes of youth gambling were. The raw data in a tabular form is listed along with a graphical view of the data for each survey question.

    Sports/Race Betting is Most Popular

    Our survey found that 26% of people, the most in the survey, found sports/race betting the most appealing.

    ● This may be because of how accessible it is.
    ● These platforms all have mobile applications that users can access and fund directly from their bank account in seconds.
    ● The accessibility, ease of use and ability to access at any time of the day make this a form of gambling that parents and younger people must be aware of.
    ● This is also the form of gambling that most begin with, our survey found that 27% of people start with sports/race betting.
