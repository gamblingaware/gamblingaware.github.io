    Initiate
    These are validated established helplines that can support a person going through a tough time. These websites contain links to helpful call lines or live chat to support people who require a more face-to-face approach.

    GAMBLE AWARE

    Gamble Aware NSW is a great gambling awareness website to utilise support chats to rapidly decrease any doubts or issues with gambling complications. The website contains real stories and videos from people that have suffered through gambling problems and the steps they have to taken to undergo a process of eradicating that problem.

    GAMBLER'S HELP

    This website is delineated towards persons living in rural areas and its geographically based solution offer a wide array of support to people living in poorer socio-economic areas or secluded areas. Gambling is often a fun pursuit and can influence the psychological state. This website provides solutions on the basis of identifying your problems and taking steps to eliminate them.

    BE GAMBLE AWARE

    This website is the most comprehensive and well known website that utilises a variety of tools to support individuals and groups with their problems with gambling. This website's helpline is not an Australian call line, however, their resources and the structure of the website is something to elevate towards your desire for education and rehabilitation.

    LIFELINE

    Lifeline is an overall crisis website that helps people in need of various types of support. The gambling support is vast and is dedicated towards those who may have visited this website before. The offer great statistics to help advocate awareness and education on the topic of gambling.

    AIFS

    AIFS prioritises gambling help as an established pillar of its support. It offers the National Gambling Helpline number and provides a link to online counselling for real time chat, online chat, and forums.
